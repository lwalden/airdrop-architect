## Overview

At Airdrop Architect, we respect your privacy rights. This page explains how you can exercise your rights under applicable privacy laws including the EU General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR), UK GDPR, and California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA).

---

## Your Rights

### All Users

Regardless of your location, you have the right to:

| Right | Description |
|-------|-------------|
| **Access** | Request a copy of your personal data |
| **Correction** | Request correction of inaccurate data |
| **Deletion** | Request deletion of your personal data |
| **Portability** | Receive your data in a machine-readable format |

### Additional Rights for EU/UK Residents

| Right | Description |
|-------|-------------|
| **Restrict Processing** | Limit how we use your data |
| **Object to Processing** | Object to processing based on legitimate interests |
| **Withdraw Consent** | Withdraw consent at any time |
| **Lodge a Complaint** | File a complaint with your data protection authority |

### Additional Rights for California Residents

| Right | Description |
|-------|-------------|
| **Right to Know** | Know what personal information we collect and how we use it |
| **Right to Delete** | Request deletion of your personal information |
| **Right to Opt-Out** | Opt out of the "sale" of personal information (we do not sell data) |
| **Non-Discrimination** | We will not discriminate against you for exercising your rights |

## Verification

To protect your privacy, we must verify your identity before processing requests.

**We may ask for:**
- Confirmation of your email address
- Confirmation of your Telegram account
- Verification of wallet ownership (if requesting data related to specific wallets)

**We will NOT ask for:**
- Private keys
- Seed phrases
- Passwords
- Payment card numbers

⚠️ **Never share your private keys or seed phrases with anyone, including us.**

## What We Can Delete

Upon verified request, we can delete:
- Your account and profile information
- Stored wallet addresses
- Usage data and preferences
- Subscription history (except as required for tax/legal records)

**What we may retain:**
- Payment transaction records (7 years for tax/legal requirements)
- Data necessary for legal claims or disputes
- Anonymized/aggregated data that cannot identify you

## Data Export (Portability)

You can request a copy of your data in a portable format. We will provide:
- Account information
- Stored wallet addresses
- Subscription history
- Usage preferences

Format: JSON or CSV (your choice)

## Right to Object

You may object to processing based on our legitimate interests. We will stop processing unless we can demonstrate compelling legitimate grounds that override your interests.

**Note:** You can always object to direct marketing. We will stop immediately.

## Frequently Asked Questions

**Q: How long will it take to delete my data?**
A: We aim to process deletion requests within 30 days. You will receive confirmation once complete.

**Q: Can I delete my data but keep my subscription active?**
A: No. Deleting your account data will cancel your subscription. We cannot provide the service without storing your account information.

**Q: Will deleting my data remove me from blockchain records?**
A: No. We can only delete data we store. Blockchain transactions are immutable and cannot be deleted by anyone.

**Q: What happens to my data if I stop paying?**
A: Free tier access continues with limited features. If you want data deleted, you must explicitly request it.

**Q: Can I request data about a specific wallet address only?**
A: Yes. Specify which wallet address(es) in your request.
